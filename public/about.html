Our Story

Founded in 2019 by chef Lila Moreno, Sunstone Bistro began as a small supper club celebrating seasonal produce and simple techniques. Over the years we've partnered with nearby farms and purveyors to bring fresh, sustainable ingredients straight to your plate.

Our Philosophy

We believe in food that honors the seasons and the people who grow it. Menus change regularly to reflect what's freshest and most flavorful. Whether you're joining us for a weeknight dinner or a weekend celebration, expect care in every bite.
